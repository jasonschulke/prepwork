Featured Project

Learning Management & Certification for Kodif

The Challenge

Kodif, an AI-powered customer service platform, needed to solve two problems: educating customers on how to get the most from their platform, and improving the quality of content used to train their agentic AI support agents.

The Solution

We built a learning management system and product certification course that served dual purposes:

  • Learning Management System (LMS) with structured courses
  • Product certification program to validate customer expertise
  • Comprehensive product documentation
  • Training content designed to also serve as source material for AI agent training
  • Interactive product guides with embedded videos

The Impact

  • Improved AI agent training with higher-quality source content
  • Reduced customer onboarding time through structured learning paths
  • Increased product knowledge via certification programs
  • Scalable education infrastructure that grows with the customer base
App Development

Custom Web App for Center Stage Floors

The Challenge

Center Stage Floors, a modular flooring manufacturer, was struggling with disconnected systems. Design specifications, quoting, and production data lived in separate tools, leading to manual re-entry, errors, and delays throughout the order lifecycle.

The Solution

We developed a custom web application that integrated design, quoting, and production data into a unified workflow:

  • Integrated design specification capture
  • Automated quote generation from design inputs
  • Seamless handoff to production with all data intact
  • Real-time order tracking and status updates
  • Centralized dashboard for operations visibility

The Impact

  • Automated workflows across design, quoting, and production
  • Reduced manual input and data re-entry errors
  • Improved order accuracy throughout the lifecycle
  • Single source of truth for the entire operation

Platform Development

Global Campaign Management for Levi's

The Challenge

Levi's marketing teams across the globe were managing product and creative assets in silos. With over 100,000 assets spread across regions and teams, finding the right content and maintaining brand consistency was a significant challenge.

The Solution

We built a global campaign management platform that unified all assets and streamlined marketing operations:

  • Centralized asset library with 100K+ product and creative files
  • Advanced search and filtering by campaign, region, and product line
  • Role-based access for regional marketing teams
  • Approval workflows for brand consistency
  • Integration with existing marketing tools and workflows

The Impact

  • Unified asset management across all regions and teams
  • Streamlined marketing operations with faster campaign launches
  • Improved brand consistency through centralized governance
  • Reduced duplication and asset recreation costs
